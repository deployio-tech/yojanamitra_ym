Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

1. Introduction

YojanaMitra ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

By using YojanaMitra, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, mobile number
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, occupation, income, caste, state, education, marital status, disability status, residence type
  • Family Information: Father's occupation, mother's occupation, family type

2.2 Document Information

  • Uploaded Documents: Aadhaar Card, Ration Card, Income Certificate, Caste Certificate, Marks Card, and other government documents
  • Extracted Data: Information extracted from documents using AI (Google Gemini)

2.3 Usage Information

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent
  • Document Access Logs: When and which documents were accessed

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Scheme Matching: To match you with eligible government schemes
  • Document Verification: To verify your documents using AI-powered OCR
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account
  • Communication: To send you scheme notifications and updates
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage and improve our platform

4. AI Document Processing

Third-Party AI Service: We use Google Gemini AI to extract information from your uploaded documents.

Important:
  • Documents are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing
  • Google does NOT permanently store your documents (per their API terms)
  • Processing happens in real-time and data is discarded after extraction
  • We encrypt all documents on our servers

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All uploaded documents are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (Fernet/AES-256)
  • HTTPS: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Access Control: Only you can access your documents (authenticated access only)
  • Audit Logs: We maintain logs of all document access for security purposes

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Inactive Account Cleanup:

If you do not log in for 30 consecutive days, your uploaded documents will be automatically deleted for security purposes. Your account profile will remain active.

Manual Deletion: You can delete your documents or entire account at any time from your dashboard.

7. Your Rights (DPDPA 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@yojanamitra.in

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data.

We only share data with:

  • Google Gemini AI: For document OCR processing (temporary, not stored)
  • Email Service (SendGrid): For sending notifications
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or court order

9. Cookies

We use session cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your language preference
  • Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

YojanaMitra is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@yojanamitra.in
  • Support: support@yojanamitra.in
